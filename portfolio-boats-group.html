Boats Group

Services Project - This is my latest project as a lead designer. As part of a platform that displays boats for sale, this project was focused on creating a marketplace to sell services (shipping, warranty, finance, etc), tailored for the marine industry.


Project Background


The Services page of the tree portals is highly accessed, and both users and business would benefit from a more reliable landing page displaying all the services provided. With more than 1.8M views each month, the possibility of returning users engaging with this page could directly affect the company's revenue.

This project took me about 3 months, from Ideation to the hands-off part with high fidelity designs. I had to organize the pages flow, responsiveness, user experience and lead forms.

Role:

UX/UI Designer


Before

Goals and Objectives

The goal of this project was to redesign and recreate the "services page" experience, to encourage more clicks from visitors that already use the 3 main portals in search of boats for sale, and provide them with a range of services. It was a valuable up-selling opportunity that needed an intuitive design, centered on the users profile and matching each portal identity.






Results

Each portal now has its own Services page, designed in harmony with each brand identity, taking in consideration the demographics of the users and business requirements.
