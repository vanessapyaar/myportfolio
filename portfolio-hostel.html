Eco Hostel Project

The hostel website was first built in 2013. With all the web changes and rapid innovations throughout the years, they decided to change the look and feel of the site, back in 2015. The main goal was to improve the user experience to generate more leads and direct bookings. Also, they wanted to adjust the branding to better match their client's profile.


The Problem

The hospitality field faces a big challenge when it comes to revenue generation. The big hotel chains retain most of the market share, hence small business needs to rely on third-party vendors to generate bookings, which costs about 18% of each reservation. Consequently one of the pain points was the low rate of direct bookings.

Process and Approaches

During the briefing, I analyzed the data on google analytics to evaluate metrics such as bounce rate, returning visitors, and the general engagement with the booking page. The first thing and most obvious, was the lack of responsiveness for mobile devices. With heatmaps, we were able to understand that users were clicking in low conversion elements, and not paying attention to the booking area.
