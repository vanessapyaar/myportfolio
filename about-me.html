About Me

Passionate about user-centered design and technology, I have 8 years of experience as a Web Designer.
I started as a freelancer, worked with small and medium businesses, and now it has been two years since I’m working with a corporation in Miami, full-time. This diversity gives me a broad understanding of different management styles and how to work with big/small teams. In the coorporation environment I learned how to pair and communicate well with developers, engineers, and product owners.  

In the last 2 years I’ve been working with Agile software, and I love such dynamic and fast-paced environment. I'm a team player and I find it easy to communicate with others, take criticism well and refine my work when feedback is given.


My Design Process

I follow a six-step design process that begins with defining the problem, ideation, designing solutions, testing, and finally implementing my designs.

My methodology beings by defining the problem I am going to solve. I emphatize with users and develop a clear context of the pain points from their perspective.


Next, I transition to the ideation stage, where I brainstorm and explore creative solutions!

With good ideas in hand, I prototype low-fidelity designs and gather feedback to be certain the team/stakeholders agree with the direction. Based on critiques and observations, I develop the high-fidelity variations.

Next I design tests to gauge the effectiveness of my final designs. This is an essential stage that requires an open mind. The question I want to answer is, "Do the designs achieve the business goal and engage the user?" Test results can be fun and illuminating because they may give counter intuitive results... This is my desgin approach in a nutshell.
