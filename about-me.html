About Me

Passionate about user-centered design and technology, I have 8 years of experience as a Designer.
I started as a freelancer, worked with small and medium businesses, now it has been two years since I’m working with a corporation in Miami, full-time. This diversity gives me a broad understanding of different management styles and how to work with big/small teams. The coorporation environment taught me how to pair and communicate well with developers, engineers, and product owners.  

In the last 2 years I’ve been working with Agile software, and I love such dynamic and fast-paced environment. I'm a team player and I find it easy to communicate with others, take criticism well and refine my work when feedback is given.
