Hi, I'm Vanessa.

A Designer (UX, UI, HTML, CSS) based in Miami. I think good design is usable, simple, attractive, and is ultimately the result of a designer with a thorough understanding of the final user.


My Design Process

I follow a six-step design process that begins with defining the problem, ideation, designing solutions, testing, and finally implementing my designs.

My methodology beings by defining the problem I am going to solve. I emphatize with users and develop a clear context of the pain points from their perspective.


Next, I transition to the ideation stage, where I brainstorm and explore creative solutions!

With good ideas in hand, I prototype low-fidelity designs and gather feedback to be certain the team/stakeholders agree with the direction. Based on critiques and observations, I develop the high-fidelity variations.

Next I design tests to gauge the effectiveness of the high fidelity designs. This is an essential stage that requires an open mind. The question I want to answer is, "Do the designs achieve the business goal and engage the user?" Test results can be fun and illuminating because they may give counter intuitive results...

With the high fidelity designs in hand, the next important step is testing. Adopting an open mind is essential in this stage. When the results are in, it's time to ask: what did we learn? Does our solution engage the user? Do we need to fine tune the designs? Test results can be fun and illuminating because they may give counter intuitive results... If we have a winner the last step would be implementing the designs, if not, then the iteration cycle starts again.
